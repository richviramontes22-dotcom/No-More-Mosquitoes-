No More Mosquitoes Session Status Report
========================================

1. Executive Summary
--------------------
- The home experience now ships with branded layout chrome, structured data, pricing logic, testimonials, FAQs, schedule/contact CTAs, and reusable section primitives tailored to the NMM identity.
- React Query, toast stacks, and tooltip providers wrap the SPA so future pages can reuse data-fetching and feedback systems without reconfiguration.
- Secondary sitemap routes (pricing, services, story, etc.) still surface the shared "Coming Soon" placeholder, keeping navigation intact while signaling incomplete work.
- Key follow-ups include restoring the missing `HeroSection` import in `client/pages/Index.tsx`, swapping placeholder logic for finished pages, and integrating real address, scheduling, and analytics services.

2. Implemented Experience
-------------------------
### 2.1 Global Application Structure
- **Routing & Providers:** `client/App.tsx` wires QueryClient, tooltip/toast systems, SPA routing, and enumerates every planned route, delegating unfinished ones to `PlaceholderPage`.
- **Layout Shell:** `client/components/layout/MainLayout.tsx` centralizes the header, footer, and `main` skip target for accessibility.
- **Navigation & Branding:** `SiteHeader` and `SiteFooter` expose navigation groups, contact CTAs, and the reusable `LogoCutout` component to keep the brand consistent.

### 2.2 Home Route Composition
- `client/pages/Index.tsx` assembles the hero, address checker, quote widget, plan cards, benefits, services, pest grid, video proof, reviews, story, FAQ, schedule, and contact sections. (Hero import currently missing.)
- Section components under `client/components/sections/` provide focused, reusable UI blocks powered by data objects from `client/data/site.ts` and imagery from `client/data/media.ts`.
- `client/components/common/SectionHeading.tsx` keeps typography, eyebrow treatments, and alignment consistent across sections.

### 2.3 Data, Utilities, and Testing
- `client/lib/pricing.ts` converts acreage, cadence, and program selections into per-visit, monthly, and annual totals; `client/lib/pricing.spec.ts` covers representative tier boundaries.
- SEO metadata is managed by `client/components/seo/Seo.tsx` with structured data definitions under `client/seo/structuredData.ts`.
- Brand theming relies on Tailwind tokens in `tailwind.config.ts` and CSS variables in `client/global.css`.
- Launch-readiness tasks sit in `client/data/handoff.ts` as a checklist for infrastructure, analytics, content, QA, and support.

### 2.4 Placeholder & Error Handling
- `client/pages/Placeholder.tsx` powers every unfinished route with Coming Soon messaging, dual CTAs, and external link support.
- `client/pages/NotFound.tsx` offers the branded 404 experience plus console logging for missing routes.
